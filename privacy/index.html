Privacy Policy

Last updated May 25, 2026

This personal website, portfolio, projects page, and blog are operated by Natalie Johanek. This policy explains what information may be collected when you visit the site or contact me.

Information I collect

I do not ask you to create an account, and I do not intentionally collect personal information through forms on this site.

  • If you email me, I receive your email address, name or handle, and any information you choose to include in the message.
  • The site may use basic technical logs through hosting and infrastructure providers, such as IP address, browser type, pages requested, and timestamps.
  • The site stores your light or dark theme preference in your browser using local storage. That preference stays on your device.

How I use information

I use information only for ordinary site operations, including responding to messages, maintaining the website, improving content, preventing abuse, and keeping the site secure.

Cookies, analytics, and third parties

I do not run advertising trackers or sell visitor information. This site may load assets from third-party services such as GitHub Pages and Google Fonts, and those services may receive technical request information under their own policies. External links, including GitHub, LinkedIn, and blog references, are governed by the privacy policies of those sites.

Sharing information

I do not sell or rent personal information. I may share information only with service providers needed to operate the site, if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of the site or others.

Retention

Email correspondence may be kept as long as needed to respond, maintain records, or follow up on the conversation. Technical logs are handled by the site's hosting and infrastructure providers according to their retention practices.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13, and I do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email nat.johanek@gmail.com.

Changes
